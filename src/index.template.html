FaceTrace Offline

Local face similarity comparison for selected images.

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data leaves your device.
Probabilistic visual comparison, not identity verification.

Results are not suitable for legal, forensic, employment, access-control, or definitive identity decisions. Treat the percentage as a local similarity signal only.

Percentage method

The app aligns each detected face, extracts a 256-number ArcFace descriptor, and compares descriptors with cosine similarity. The percentage uses 100 / (1 + exp(-12 * (cosine - 0.32))), so cosine 0.32 maps to 50%. Raw cosine similarity and Euclidean distance appear only in technical details.
